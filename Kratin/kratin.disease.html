Common Diseases in Elderly People

Arthritis

Arthritis is the inflammation of one or more joints, causing pain and stiffness. Common types include osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.

know more

Osteoporosis

Osteoporosis is a condition characterized by weak and brittle bones, increasing the risk of fractures. It is more common in postmenopausal women.

Know More

Hypertension

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a chronic condition that can lead to heart disease, stroke, and other health complications. It is important to manage blood pressure levels.

Know More

Diabetes

Diabetes is a metabolic disorder characterized by high blood sugar levels. It requires careful management of diet, exercise, and medication.

Know More

Dementia

Dementia refers to a group of cognitive disorders that affect memory, thinking, and behavior. Alzheimer's disease is the most common form of dementia.
